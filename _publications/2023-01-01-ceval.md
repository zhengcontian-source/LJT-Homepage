## Abstract

C-Eval is a comprehensive Chinese evaluation suite designed for foundation models, featuring multi-level and multi-discipline assessment tasks. This benchmark provides a standardized framework for evaluating the capabilities of large language models on Chinese language understanding across diverse academic disciplines.
